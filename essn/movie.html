The Best Movies According to Sameer Kumar (meee)

My top 3 favourite movies of all-time.

Cars 2006/ Cars 4

This is my favourite animated Family/Comedy movie. I just can't forget the amazing script-plot and crazy graphics/animations.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (HP Series)

The best and never-forgetable Fantasy-Fiction movie series I have ever fell in love with and was not bored at all.

The Family Rockstar

One of the few Rom-Com movies that I adore alot, though for many its a simple rom-com, but for me its was super emotion bringing (relatable) and I also did like a similar movie Geeta Govindham.
